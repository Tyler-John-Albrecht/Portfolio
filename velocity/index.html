Answers before you ask

Frequently asked questions

How much does a full color-change wrap cost?
It depends on vehicle size, finish, and complexity — exotic finishes like color-flip or chrome run higher than standard gloss. What we promise: you get an exact number for YOUR car, in writing, before we book anything. No ranges, no surprises on pickup.
Will a wrap damage my factory paint?
The opposite — the film shields your paint from rock chips, UV fade, and light scratches. On healthy factory paint, a professionally installed and removed wrap leaves the finish exactly as we found it. That's why lease drivers love them.
How long does a wrap last?
Quality film professionally installed lasts 5–7 years outdoors, longer if garaged. We only install 3M, XPEL, Avery Dennison, KPMF, and Inozetek — films with real manufacturer warranties behind them.
How long is my car in the shop?
Five business days for a full wrap: two for prep and disassembly, two for install, one for inspection and reassembly. Rushing any of those steps is how edges lift — so we don't.
Can you wrap just part of my car?
Absolutely — roofs, hoods, mirrors, chrome delete, and racing stripes are some of our most-booked jobs, and most are done in a single day. Ask for a partial-wrap quote when you call.
