Honest answers

What people ask before booking

Does eco-friendly actually clean as well?
Better, in most cases. Steam at 300°F dissolves grime that soap just pushes around, kills bacteria without bleach, and reaches vents and seams a rag never will. "Eco" is how we clean — spotless is still the standard.
How long does a full detail take?
The Refresh runs about 90 minutes. The Full Steam is half a day. The Evergreen with ceramic coating is a full day, because coating cures on our clock — not the calendar's.
Can you really get smoke smell out?
Yes — and not by masking it. Steam extraction pulls residue out of the fabric, then ozone treatment breaks down the odor molecules themselves. It's the difference between covering a smell and removing it.
Do I need to bring anything?
Just the car and the keys. Clear out anything valuable or personal beforehand — everything else (mats, seats, trunk) is our job.
